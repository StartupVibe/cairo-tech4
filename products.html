06

The jib crane or pivot winch is used in fixed locations and does not move because it is fixed to a concrete base according to the winch load, where the loads start from 250 kg up to 5 tons, provided that the rotation angle is manual or electric in different degrees from 180 degrees up to 360 degrees.

08

The monorail crane is a type of single-beam overhead crane, but it operates on a single longitudinal or transverse path, meaning it serves only one path in both directions. Sometimes it is circular or semi-circular. This type is used in warehouses and chlorine rooms in water treatment plants.
